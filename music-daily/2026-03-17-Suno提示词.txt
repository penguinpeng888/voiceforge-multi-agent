# Suno 音乐提示词

## 完整提示词 (英文)

**Style & Mood:**
Melancholic electronic pop with cinematic atmosphere. Emotional, introspective, late-night vibes. A blend of synthwave and alternative pop, with a dreamy and reflective mood. The song should feel like driving alone through a rainy city at 3am.

**Instrumentation:**
- Primary: Synthesizers with warm pads
- Rhythm: Soft electronic drums with subtle bass
- Melody: Piano chords layered with synth leads
- Texture: Ambient atmospheric sounds, gentle rain samples
- Guitar: Clean electric guitar with reverb for emotional moments

**Structure:**
- Intro: Atmospheric synth pad with soft piano (8 bars)
- Verse: Mellow rhythm, building emotion (16 bars)
- Pre-chorus: Slight build-up, increasing intensity (8 bars)
- Chorus: Full synth drop, emotionally powerful (16 bars)
- Verse 2: Return to mellow, adding depth (16 bars)
- Pre-chorus: Build again (8 bars)
- Chorus: Full chorus repeat with variations (16 bars)
- Bridge: Stripped down, piano and vocals only (8 bars)
- Outro: Fading synths, dreamy conclusion (8 bars)

**Tempo:** 85-95 BPM (mid-tempo, reflective)
**Key:** Minor key (Am or Em) for melancholic feel
**Vocal Style:** Soft, breathy, emotional delivery. Male or female voice with intimate, confessional tone.

## 简化提示词 (中文)

**风格:** 忧郁电子流行，赛博朋克氛围，深夜独白风格

**情绪:** 孤独、反思、怀旧、雨夜都市感

**乐器:**
- 主旋律: 温暖合成器PAD + 钢琴
- 节奏: 轻柔电子鼓 + 低音贝斯
- 氛围: 环境音+雨声采样
- 吉他: 清爽电吉他带混响

**结构:** 前奏(8拍) → 主歌(16拍) → 副歌(16拍) → 间奏 → 副歌(16拍) → 桥段(8拍) → 尾奏(8拍)

**速度:** 85-95 BPM
**调式:** 小调 (Am或Em)
**人声:** 轻柔、气声、情感充沛

## 完整Suno提示词 (可直接使用)

```
[Verse]
Soft synth pads with gentle piano chords establish a late-night atmosphere. Electronic drums enter subtly. Melancholic melody with emotional depth.

[Pre-Chorus]
Building intensity. Synth layers increase. Bass deepens. Anticipation builds toward the chorus.

[Chorus]
Full synth drop with powerful emotional release. Warm pads, driving drums, and melodic hooks. Cinematic and anthemic yet intimate.

[Verse 2]
Return to the mellow verse texture. Adding subtle layers. Deepening the emotional narrative.

[Bridge]
Stripped down. Piano and vocal only. The most intimate moment of the song.

[Outro]
Fading synths. Rain samples. Dreamy conclusion. The sound of a city at 3am.

Style: Synthwave, Alternative Pop, Electronic, Cinematic Pop
Mood: Melancholic, Dreamy, Emotional, Late Night, Rainy City
Tempo: 90 BPM
Key: A Minor
Vocals: Soft, Breathy, Intimate, Emotional
Instruments: Synthesizers, Piano, Electronic Drums, Bass, Ambient Textures
```

## 使用建议

1. **首选英文提示词** - Suno对英文支持更好
2. **可调整速度** - 想要更舒缓用85 BPM，想要更有力用95 BPM
3. **人声选择** - 选择"male vocal, soft, emotional"或"female vocal, breathy, dreamy"
4. **参考曲目:** The Midnight - 《Sunset》、FM-84 - 《Running in the Night》、Gunship - 《Tech Noir》
