提示词1（主风格：古风民谣）：
A mellow Chinese folk song with traditional instruments - guzheng, pipa, and erhu melody over acoustic guitar. Soft female vocals singing about misty Jiangnan. Tempo 75 BPM, key of D major. Warm nostalgic atmosphere with subtle jazz influences. Cinematic arrangement with gentle percussion.

提示词2（爵士+古风融合）：
Jazz-infused Chinese pop-folk fusion. Smooth saxophone solo intertwined with traditional Chinese flute. Urban coffee shop vibe meets ancient water town romance. Acoustic guitar rhythm with light brush drums. Dreamy synth pads underneath. Lyrical male vocal, emotional and soulful. 4/4 time, tempo 85 BPM.

提示词3（现代古风电子元素）：
Modern Chinese indie folk with electronic ambient textures. Ethereal guzheng plucks over lo-fi hip hop beat. Rain sounds and field recordings from water towns. Nostalgic melodies that feel like old memories. Atmospheric and cinematic. Soft female vocal with reverb. Tempo 90 BPM, emotional and touching.
